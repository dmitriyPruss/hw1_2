Top 3 heavyweight boxers

Boxers who weigh over 200 pounds (91 kg; 14 st 4 lb) are considered heavyweights by 3 of the 4 major professional boxing organizations: the International Boxing Federation ,[1] the World Boxing Association,[2] and the World Boxing Organization.[3] In 2020, the World Boxing Council [4] increased their heavyweight classification to 224 pounds to allow for their creation of the bridgerweight division.

  1. Tyson Fury

  2. Anthony Joshua

  3. Deontay Wilder
